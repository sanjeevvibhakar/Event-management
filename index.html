About Us

RACAF is the vibrant cultural extravaganza held at NERIST, celebrating the rich diversity of art, music, dance, and traditions. It’s a platform where students come together to showcase their talents, express creativity, and embrace the spirit of unity. From mesmerizing performances to exciting competitions, RACAF offers an unforgettable experience, making it the heartbeat of NERIST's cultural life!
